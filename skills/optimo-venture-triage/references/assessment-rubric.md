# Reference — Assessment Rubric

Score each dimension **0–5** (0 = absent/fatal, 3 = acceptable, 5 = excellent), attach a
**confidence 0.00–1.00**, and cite evidence (workspace page titles and/or external sources).
The weighted overall (0–100) feeds the gate. Weights are defaults a founder can tune.

| # | Dimension | Weight | What "5" looks like | Key evidence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Strategic fit & overlap | 20% | Advances the portfolio thesis; no duplication of an existing venture/sidecar; clear leverage of the platform | Canonical Registry (01.04), existing hubs under 05.02, sidecars |
| 2 | Ecosystem placement | 15% | One obvious, defensible home (A/B/C); clean dependencies on Codex/Cortex/Agile | Layer 01/02/03 pages; target venture/sidecar |
| 3 | Risk | 20% | Low/again-mitigated technical, compliance, security, IP, execution risk | 01.03 Compliance Canon; E&O/Cyber/Regulatory Insurance Framework; PGAS/NSQM safeguards |
| 4 | Cost | 15% | Modest build + run + model/inference cost; disciplined token/infra footprint | 01.01.10 Token Discipline Canon; Nebius integration canon |
| 5 | Benefit / value | 15% | Strong revenue model + platform reuse + durable moat | Thesis, comparable venture economics |
| 6 | Market | 10% | Large/growing TAM, clear demand, defensible timing, manageable competition | **Live web research** (see below) |
| 7 | Feasibility & readiness | 5% | Buildable on Lovable + Supabase + Vercel; data/dependencies available now | App Build Standard; data availability |

**Scoring rules**
- A dimension scored **0–2 on Risk or Strategic fit** is a candidate **blocking condition** — call it out explicitly; it can force NO-GO regardless of the weighted total.
- Every score needs a one-line justification and at least one evidence pointer. No evidence → lower the confidence, don't inflate the score.
- Report the weighted overall AND the lowest-confidence dimensions, so the human sees where the assessment is shakiest.

**Overlap / cannibalization check (do this every time)**
1. Search the Canonical Registry and existing hub titles for adjacent ventures.
2. Search sidecars for adjacent enrichment scope.
3. If material overlap exists, the honest dispositions are usually B (sidecar/extend) or D
   (merge into the existing venture), not C. Say which existing entity it overlaps and how much.

## Live market analysis (web research)

For dimension 6, use web search/fetch to ground the market view in current external data:
- Estimate **TAM / SAM / SOM** with sourced figures; note the date and source of each figure.
- Identify **direct and adjacent competitors**, their positioning, and any recent funding/launches.
- Assess **timing and demand signal** (regulatory shifts, search/interest trends, analyst views).
- **Cite sources** and prefer primary/reputable ones. Paraphrase; quote sparingly and briefly.
- If evidence is thin or conflicting, lower the dimension's confidence and say so — don't overclaim.

Keep the market section proportionate (a few well-sourced paragraphs), not a full report.
